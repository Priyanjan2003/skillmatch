About matchMYskills

matchMYskills is a smart job matching platform that helps candidates find the right jobs based on their skills or resume. It removes irrelevant job listings and shows only meaningful matches.

How to Use matchMYskills

  • 1. Complete Your Profile
    Add your skills, experience, and upload your resume.
  • 2. Choose Matching Mode
    Select Skill-Based matching or Resume (ATS) matching.
  • 3. View Job Matches
    See jobs with clear skill match counts like 2/4 skills matched.
  • 4. Apply Confidently
    Apply only where your profile genuinely fits the job.
